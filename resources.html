Shukran

"Shukr" as a noun means "Thanks (noun)", so when you want to say "Thank you" to someone you'd normally say "Shukran Lak" ("Lak" is equivalent of "you" in this context), but you can use just the word "Shukran" if you want and it'll keep the same meaning, just like when you say "Thanks!" to somebody in english.
